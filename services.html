Mission Statement

1 Coast Media exists to grow the Mississippi Gulf Coast by helping the businesses that power it grow.

Our mission is simple:

  • Help local businesses get more customers through modern marketing, UGC, AI, and smart content.
  • Empower local creators with real, paid opportunities to do what they love.
  • Increase tourism and visibility through authentic storytelling and professional media.
  • Strengthen the entire Coast by uniting cities, businesses, and creators into one shared ecosystem.

We are not here to compete with the Coast, we are here to elevate it. Every project, every campaign, every collaboration has one goal: to make the Mississippi Gulf Coast stronger, louder, more connected, and more prosperous.

This is the heart behind everything we do.

From Our Founder

Growing up on the Mississippi Gulf Coast shaped everything about who I am. The people, the small businesses, the food, the beaches, the music, the culture.. it’s all part of my identity. I’ve always believed the Gulf Coast has more potential than it’s ever been given credit for. We have some of the best local businesses, the hardest‑working people, and a community spirit that’s impossible to fake.

But I also know how hard it is to grow a business here. I know how much owners struggle with visibility, marketing, content, and keeping up with a world that’s changing faster than ever. Too many amazing businesses stay hidden. Too many great ideas never reach the people who would love them. Too many owners feel like they’re doing everything alone.

I created 1 Coast Media because I believe that when our businesses grow, our entire Coast grows. Every new customer, every new tourist, every new opportunity creates a ripple effect that touches families, neighborhoods, cities, and the entire region. I don’t want to be another agency selling random services — I want to be the engine that helps the Gulf Coast rise together.

I also believe in creators — the teenagers, moms, photographers, videographers, and everyday people with talent and a story to tell. I believe they deserve a path to earn income doing what they love while uplifting the community they live in. When local businesses and local creators work together, something powerful happens: the Gulf Coast gets louder, stronger, and more united.

This is more than business for me. This is home. This is purpose. This is legacy.

Thank you for believing in the vision of One Coast — one community, one economy, one digital voice, rising together.

— Blake Daniels, Founder of 1 Coast Media
