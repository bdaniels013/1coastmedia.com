# **Which Offer Should You Choose?**

## ⭐ **Choose Offer 1 if…**

You need a simple, affordable system that gets your business online, visible, and ready for customers.

## ⭐ **Choose Offer 2 if…**

You want consistent weekly customers, bookings, or sales and want your marketing fully done‑for‑you.

## ⭐ **Choose Offer 3 if…**

You need high‑quality, professional content that elevates your brand image.

## ⭐ **Choose Offer 4 if…**

You want to sell statewide or nationwide with funnels, automations, and UGC‑driven ads.

## ⭐ **Choose the Growth Starter  if…**

You want **full‑service marketing at a higher level** — including a custom website, SEO, content, PR, and two monthly UGC campaigns — and you need a team to manage it all for you.

Ideal for:

- Busy business owners
- Restaurants, boutiques, salons, med spas
- Businesses needing **complete monthly support**

## ⭐ **Choose the Growth Machine  if…**

You want **deeper marketing infrastructure**, more content, more UGC, advanced ecommerce, analytics, and stronger automation — a true systemized marketing engine.

Ideal for:

- Growing brands
- Multi‑location businesses
- Service providers wanting scale
- Businesses preparing to expand statewide

## ⭐ **Choose the Growth Accelerator  if…**

You’re ready for **enterprise‑level marketing**, premium production, global PR options, heavy monthly content, advanced AI/automation, and 4 UGC campaigns each month.

Ideal for:

- High‑growth brands
- Ambitious ecommerce companies
- Businesses expanding regionally, nationally, or globally
- Anyone wanting a full outsourced media and marketing department

## ⭐ **Choose Offer 5 if…**

You’re a city, county, chamber, casino, or tourism board looking to drive regional visibility and economic growth.
